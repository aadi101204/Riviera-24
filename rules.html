General Rules

You are not locked in. Let us know if you need to leave at any time.

All phones except one per team need to be left outside the room on the table. Bags as well.

You have 15 minutes to escape.

Do not share anything regarding the puzzles, hints or clues to others.

Be careful handling props; any damages will be charged to the team.

Once you have found a clue, please put back the props that you have used in their original place.

We will give you some hints, however you will be charged with a penalty of 20 sec for each hint.
